Customer Support Engineer

Customer Support Engineer

permanent, full time.

Salary: negotiable

Job Description

We’re looking for a talented customer support representative to help support websites and applications for our clients as well as other in-house creations such as our flagship solution, a fundraising platform for charities and NGOs.

Our team of web developers and DevOps engineers work across the entire stack, creating innovative and pragmatic solutions that allow the websites, web and mobile applications we create and maintain perform reliably at scale.

You’ll work collaboratively with our team and customers, dealing with incoming issues and ensuring that customers receive a prompt response to their queries.

Responsibilities

  • Being the initial point of contact for customers
  • Collaborating with other team members, sharing ideas and finding solutions
  • Juggling bugs and feature requests according to business and customer priorities
  • Responding to customer questions

Skills you have

  • An understanding of HTML, CSS and Javascript and web systems
  • An understanding of web based CMSes (e.g. you've used WordPress or a similar tool)

Nice to have/willing to learn

  • Basic understanding of Linux

About Hello Future

We’re a small and dynamic company with plenty of interesting clients and challenging internal projects. We have customers but we’re more of a consultancy than an agency like setting. Our customers tend to listen to us and while you always need to be pragmatic in business, we implement the best technical solution wherever possible.

Everyone at Hello Future needs to:
  • Agree that our customers should always have a positive experience when dealing with us and do everything possible to ensure that happens
  • Be upfront and honest with the team on the issues or challenges that are faced. If you think something is being done in the wrong way you should say so because your opinion matters
  • Be happy to explain the solution you’ve arrived at if someone thinks there might be a better way to do it
  • Understand that the best technical solution may not always be the most pragmatic one for our customers.
Benefits
  • Monthly Social Activities
  • 25 Days holiday + Bank Holidays
  • Gap between Christmas and New Year paid holiday
  • MacBook Pro and large screen to work on
  • Company pension scheme with up to 6% matched contribution

Location

This position is based in our Bristol office. Some working from home is cool but we find that collaboration amongst the team at the office is key to the quality of work that we produce.
